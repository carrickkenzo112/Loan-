Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our loan products and application process.

We offer four main types of loans: Personal Loans (up to $50,000), Mortgage Loans (up to $2,000,000), Business Loans (up to $5,000,000), and Student Loans (up to $150,000). Each loan type has specific terms and conditions tailored to meet different financial needs.
To be eligible, you must be at least 18 years old, have a valid government-issued ID, proof of income, and a good credit history. Specific requirements may vary by loan type. For example, mortgage loans require property details, while student loans require proof of enrollment.
All applicants need to provide a government-issued ID, proof of address (utility bill or bank statement), and proof of income (pay slips or tax returns). Additional documents may be required depending on the loan type, such as property documents for mortgages or school admission letters for student loans.
Most personal and student loans are approved within 24-48 hours after we receive all required documents. Mortgage and business loans may take 3-5 business days due to additional verification processes. We'll notify you immediately once a decision is made.
Our rates are competitive and vary by loan type: Personal Loans start at 0.88%, Mortgage Loans from 4.5%, Business Loans have flexible rates based on terms, and Student Loans up to 3%. Your specific rate will depend on your creditworthiness, loan amount, and term.
Yes, all our loans allow for early repayment without penalty. In fact, early repayment may reduce your total interest cost. You can make additional payments at any time through our online portal or by contacting customer service.
We understand financial difficulties can arise. If you anticipate missing a payment, please contact us immediately to discuss options. Late payments may incur fees and affect your credit score. We offer payment plan adjustments in genuine hardship cases.
We use bank-grade 256-bit SSL encryption for all data transmissions. Your information is stored securely in compliance with international financial regulations. We never share your data with third parties without your consent, except as required by law.
